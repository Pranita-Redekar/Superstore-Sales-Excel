<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is an Excel-based project that helps analyze and understand sales data of a retail store.
It shows important business information like total sales, average sales, number of transactions, and quantity sold in one place.
This dashboard helps management quickly understand sales performance and trends.



<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

Retail stores generate a large amount of sales data every day.
Because of this, it becomes difficult to:
-Track overall sales performance<br>
-Compare sales across regions and cities <br>
-Understand customer buying behavior <br>
-Identify monthly sales trends <br>

<h2><a class="anchor" id="dashboard"></a>Dashboard</h2>

The dashboard includes:<br>
-KPI cards for total sales, average sales, transactions, and quantity<br>
-Ship mode-wise sales analysis<br>
-Region-wise transaction comparison<br>
-Monthly quantity ordered trend<br>
-Top 5 cities by sales<br>
-Category-wise and customer-type-wise performance<br>
